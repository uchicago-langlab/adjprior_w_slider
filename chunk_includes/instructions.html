Instructions

In this experiment, you will be shown an array of five images on the screen. Your task is to indicate for each object how likely you think it is. You will give your rating by moving a slider bar on a scale from 0 to 100. A rating of 0 means you think the object is 'very unlikely', and a rating of 100 means you think it is 'very likely'. You can choose any value in between by moving the bar on the slider.

When you complete one array, hit 'continue', and you will see another array.

There are 48 items total, and a brief questionnaire at the end. You should be able to finish in about 10-15 minutes total.

To ensure that you can see all the images on the screen, please maximize your browser screen now, and close any other open windows.

Note
After you complete the experiment, you will be asked to answer some brief demographic questions. To validate your work, you will then supply your Prolific Worker ID, and generate a unique identifier which you will submit back at the Prolific page that sent you here.

Instructions
In this experiment, you will see arrays of five images on the screen. Above the images will be a description. For each array: look at all of the images, and decide how likely do you think each of the objects in the array is in the real world. Your job is provide a rating on a scale from 0 to 100. Then hit 'continue', and you will see another array. There are 48 items total, and a brief questionnaire at the end. You should be able to finish in about 10-15 minutes total. Before you begin the experiment, there will be two practice items.

Note
After you complete the experiment, you will be asked to answer some brief demographic questions. To validate your work, you will then supply your MTurk Worker ID, and generate a unique identifier which you will submit back at the MTurk page that sent you here.

Purpose of Study

This experiment is one small component of a larger research project investigating language comprehension. The information collected in this study will be used in the preparation of papers for presentation in international conferences and journals concerned with linguistics research.

Procedures

In this study, you will be presented with sets of images and asked to provide feedback on them, as directed in the experimental instructions.

The entire process will last about 10 minutes. Upon completion, you will be compensated with $1.5USD.

Risks

There are no known risks involved in this experimental procedure.

Benefits

There are no immediate benefits of this research. In the long term, however, this research may help in the design of effective methods for language instruction and treatment of language disorders.

Confidentiality

Your results will be kept completely confidential. Your name will not appear in any publication or presentation. To maximize confidentiality, your results will be assigned an arbitrary numeric identifier even for our own analyses.
